Crystals Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random "Match Score" at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your "Current Score".

You win by matching your "Current Score" to the "Match Score". You lose if your "Current Score" goes above the "Match Score".

The points of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the points of each crystal.
